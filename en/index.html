2016 Beginnings of Padova

Padova began as a dream sprouted from passion and became a reality in March 2016 in Cali, Colombia. The brand’s goal was to appeal to women worldwide as their daily choice at a fair price, a commitment they uphold to this day. The name Padova was inspired by the Italian city where the founder, Catalina Leyva, fervently asked the patron saint, San Francisco de Asis, to grant another one of her great dreams. She asked for help in finding the love of her life with whom she now shares a beautiful family.

2017 Strategic Change

After a fruitless attempt at wholesale, Catalina decides to make a pivotal change. As a last attempt at survival, she tries to sell direct-to-consumer by opening a physical locale in Cali.

2018 A Bright Future

The change is successful and Padova progresses as a brand. Seeing a promising future, Catalina opens a second store in Cali and asks her sister to join forces with her as they go after the dream together.

2019 National Expansion

The first Padova store in Colombia's capital, Bogota, is officially inaugurated. This step initiates Padova's expansion and sees the brand’s popularity expand to national heights.

2020 Trials and Tribulations

The COVID-19 pandemic puts four years of hard work at risk and gives Padova one of its most difficult years. With a strong desire to survive, Padova pivots and promptly launches a line of trendy masks for everyday use. They also unite 16 brands under the Avanti collective with the promise of mutual support and an opportunity to share digital retail space on their website.

2021 Hope on the Horizon

Padova survives the pandemic, proving the dream is still alive and well. To continue advancing, they start to look toward international opportunities for diversification. They begin to participate in fairs and events, such as the Latin American Fashion Summit: LAFS.

2022 Mexico Multi Store

Padova becomes the most recent addition in a multi-brand space called Lago in Mexico City. This space strives to introduce Latin-American design in a communal physical space.

2023 New Adventures in Spain

The new Padova store is inaugurated in a breathtaking location in Madrid's prime shopping district. The new locale is located on the famous street, Serrano, becoming their flagship store outside of Colombia. By joining forces with the famous architect and textile designer, Jorge Lizarazo, they create a space representative of Colombia’s magic, beauty, and talent. This step symbolizes the start of a new era full of adventure and opportunity.
